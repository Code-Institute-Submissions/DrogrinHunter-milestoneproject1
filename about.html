About Us!

Willmore Construction are a West Midlands based company carrying out construction services, property development and refurbishment work across the UK. With vast experience and an excellent track record, we have positioned ourselves as the ideal solution for a range of industrial and commercial development projects. We take pride in offering a first class service, and enabling our clients to maximise the potential of their properties.

All our workforce are DBS registered and carry CSCS certification and our highly skilled team pride themselves on being respectful, polite, and professional.
