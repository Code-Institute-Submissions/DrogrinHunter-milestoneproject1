Our Services!

At Willmore Construction, we have a multi talented team of skilled labourers so we are able to offer a lot of different services to our clients. These are, but not limited too:

Dilapidations and Insurance

We offer full building maintenance services for insurance companies, landlords and tenants ranging from minor repairs to full refurbishment and reinstatement schedules

Painting and Decorating

We offer fully skilled painters and decorators that will prepare all types of surfaces followed by the application of specialist paints

Office Refurbishment

We can fully refurbish office spaces from floor to ceiling including plumbing and electical works. Remodelling includes the removal and construction of any internal wall and installation of glazed panels
